# ProfRandom92

Building **CompText** — an experimental industrial AI and text-compression research system focused on deterministic transformation, benchmark evidence, and deployment-ready showcase workflows.

## Current focus

- Deterministic text compression and KVTC-style representation strategies
- Synthetic-only benchmark validation and evidence reporting
- Industrial AI demo readiness with clear safety boundaries
- FastAPI, React/Vite, Render, Vercel, and CI-backed smoke checks
- Release-health dashboards and reproducible validation workflows

## Featured work

### CompText Daimler Experiment

Industrial-style CompText showcase and benchmark environment with a FastAPI backend, React/Vite frontend, Render/Vercel deployment support, synthetic benchmark fixtures, and CI-backed demo readiness checks.

Key themes:

- Synthetic data only
- No committed secrets
- Benchmark caveats over inflated claims
- Evidence-oriented review posture
- Deployment and smoke-test automation

### CompText V7

Experimental validation line for CompText V7 concepts, release-health tracking, industrial benchmark workflows, and agent-assisted validation infrastructure.

## Engineering principles

- Prefer deterministic outputs where possible
- Separate demo data from real-world/customer data
- Keep benchmarks reproducible and caveated
- Treat safety, observability, and CI as first-class project components
- Build research prototypes that can still be reviewed, deployed, and tested
